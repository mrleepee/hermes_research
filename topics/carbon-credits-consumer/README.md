# Carbon Credits for Consumer Offsetting

Research on carbon credit platforms and mechanisms available to everyday consumers for offsetting lifestyle carbon footprint — especially air travel, driving, and home energy.

## Focus Areas

- Consumer-facing carbon offset platforms
- Flight carbon calculators and offset programs
- Lifestyle carbon tracking apps
- Direct air capture (DAC) for individuals
- Biochar and local offset projects
- Carbon credit marketplaces (Verra, Gold Standard, etc.)
- Regenerative agriculture credits
- Subscription-based offset services

## Key Questions

- How effective are consumer offsets really?
- Which platforms are most reputable/transparent?
- Cost per ton of CO2 for consumers
- Additionality and permanence concerns
- How to verify offset quality
